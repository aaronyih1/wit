wing it.

May 14th-15th

wit. is a new event where for the first time in history, UCLA’s most creative students will come together for 48 hours to find solutions to problems that plague humanity.

Participants will be split up into groups of four that will tackle a challenge associated with the year’s theme. This year, the challenge is sustainability. The winning team will be awarded seed money and mentorship to implement their proposed solution.
